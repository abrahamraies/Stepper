This is the most difficult step for me, the choice of the project to choose is something quite personal, in my case I like to choose projects that help me in my day to day, that generate a contribution to me or to society. It is also necessary to think about the complexity of the project, the ideal is to start with a MVP (minimum viable product) and then scale the project based on knowledge. Points to consider when choosing the project:
1) Does it help in my daily life?
2) Does it generate a positive impact on society? Can it help other people?
3) Can it become a service?
4) Does something similar exist? Can I improve it?
5) What is the MVP?
Once you have the idea in your head it is a good practice to try to diagram it, make the flowchart to identify what is needed and understand the project from end to end.
